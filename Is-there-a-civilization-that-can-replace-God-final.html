Is there a civilization that can replace God?
As we know, we are most likely not alone in space. There are civilizations in space.
civilizations get created/per change happening in a universe. Not only are we full of other civilizations,
but we are probably not very advanced in terms of technology, intelligence and energy. Humans have
not travelled to another planet. The furthest is the moon. We are rated as a Type 0.7 civilization in the
Kardashev Scale. We are not in the top, since it goes up to at least Type 7. Today we will be focusing on
the 7th Type. Can the 7th type be the one that started everything? And most importantly, why did they
create us?
Starting with the abilities of the 7th type, they can control a part of the multiverse. Meaning
that, they can create, edit, and delete universes. Time travelling in the 7th type could be done by
creating a universe as mentioned in my previous theory. So, even if Earth was created by the 7th type,
or to be more accurate, even if Earths were created by the 7th type, they could be all altered. So, if
the 7th type is not God, they can emulate the powers and abilities to become one.
So, the 7th type could be named God, but why did they create us? If our universe is one of the
universes made per choice, then they probably wont harm us and the role of the God of Our
Universe is nonexistent. But if they created us, they might manipulate us towards their and their
friends benefit. For example, humans have noticed some strange effects. People have sworn they have
seen items that never existed. This is called The Mandella Effect. Let me give you an example: The
Monopoly Man always wears a monocle? Well, there was never a version of the Monopoly Man with
a monocle. The 7th type might have edited it for their benefit.
